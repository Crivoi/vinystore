Shipping Info

For orders inside Romania records will be shipped via Fan Courier and Posta Romana and for outside orders for the moment only with Romanian Post Office.
You will be charged depending on the number of records you have in your cart and the zone you are located in.
Too see the shipping price for a given order, please use the shipping calculator you find in your cart. If your country is not in the list, you can always contact us and discuss other shipping options.
Please make sure you entered the correct shipping address when you ordered from us, and make sure there’s someone available to accept your package (signature required) when it arrives.
Orders that are returned to us due to an incomplete or incorrect address provided will not be returned to the customer and will only be resent at the customer’s expense.
Additionally, orders that are abandoned by the customer for any reason will not be returned to the customer and will only be resent at the customer’s expense.
Please keep in mind: If ordering on Friday after 5pm, we’ll take care of your order on Monday!!!
